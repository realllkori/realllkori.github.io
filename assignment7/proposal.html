Introduction

The aim of the final project is to create a public platform for variety show enthusiasts or professionals to share their ideas or plans on variety show programmes. The platform will include a general view on variety shows in Asian countries (mainly East Asian countries according to the retrieved data), and specific analysis on typical shows from different genres (such as Running Man, Show Me the Money and Heart Signal, etc.), to display their popular games/sections, the changes in ratings for different episodes and seasons. Finally, the website will generate a form for users to fill in to demonstrate their specific plans of a variety show.
